What are your visiting hours?Waa maxay saacadaha booqashada?

Our visiting hours are from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. Emergency services are available 24/7.Saacadaha booqashadayadu waa 8:00 subaxnimo ilaa 8:00 habeenimo maalin kasta. Adeegyada degdega ah waa la heli karaa 24/7.

Do I need an appointment?Ma u baahanahay ballansho?

While walk-ins are accepted, we recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting times and ensure personalized care.Inkastoo la aqbalayso galitaanka, waxaan ku talineynaa inaad ballansho sameyso si aad uga fogaato waqtiga sugitaanka oo aad u hubiso daryeelka shaqsiyeed.

What insurance do you accept?Waa maxay caymiska aad aqbasho?

We accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for specific coverage information.Waxaan aqbalnaa qorshayaasha caymiska ee ugu waaweyn. Fadlan la xiriir qaybta lacag-bixinta si aad u hesho macluumaad gaar ah oo ku saabsan daboolka.
