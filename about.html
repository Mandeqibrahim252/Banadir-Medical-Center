Our StorySheekadeena

Founded in 1999, Banadir Medical Center has grown from a small clinic to one of the leading healthcare facilities in Somalia. For over 25 years, we have been serving the people of Mogadishu with dedication, compassion, and medical excellence.La aasaasay 1999, Xarunta Caafimaadka Banadir waxay ka korodhay klinik yar ilaa mid ka mid ah xarumaha caafimaadka ee hoggaamiya Soomaaliya. In ka badan 25 sano, waxaan u adeegnay dadka Muqdisho oo leh dedaal, naxariis, iyo gobanimada caafimaadka.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses the latest medical technology and is staffed by a team of highly qualified specialists committed to providing personalized care for every patient.Xarumadayada casri ah waxay ku leeyihiin teknolojiyada caafimaadka ee ugu dambeeyay waxaana ka shaqeeya koox khubarooyin xirfad leh oo ku dedaalaya daryeelka shaqsiyeed ee bukaan kasta.

Mission & VisionHigsashada & Aragtiyada

Our MissionHigsashadeena

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to all people in Somalia, regardless of their background, through compassionate care and medical innovation.Inaan bixino adeegyo caafimaad oo la heli karo, qiimo jaban, oo tayo sare leh dadka Soomaaliya oo dhan, iyada oo loo marayo daryeel naxariis leh iyo horumar caafimaad.

Our VisionAragtiyadeena

To be the leading healthcare provider in East Africa, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and exceptional patient experience that improves lives.Noqo bixiyaha caafimaadka ee hoggaamiya Bariga Afrika, oo la yaqaan tayada caafimaadka, daaweynta hal-abuurka ah, iyo waayo-aragnimada bukaanka ee aan caadi ahayn ee nolosha wanaajisa.
