Frequently Asked QuestionsSu'aalaha Badanaa La Weydiyo

Find answers to common questions about our services, appointments, and insurance.Hel jawaabaha su'aalaha caadiga ah ee ku saabsan adeegyadeena.

HomeGuriga / FAQSu'aalaha

What are the hospital visiting hours?Waa maxay saacadaha booqashada?

Visiting hours are from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. For ICU and special wards, please contact the nursing station for specific visiting times.Saacadaha booqashada waa 8:00 AM ilaa 8:00 PM maalin kasta. ICU iyo wardyada gaar ah, fadlan la xiriir xarunta nurse-ka.

Do I need an appointment?Ma u baahanahay ballansho?

While walk-ins are accepted for emergencies, we recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting times and ensure personalized care from your preferred doctor.Inkastoo degdegaha la aqbalayo, waxaan ku talineynaa inaad ballansho sameyso si aad uga fogaato waqtiga sugitaanka.

What insurance plans do you accept?Waa maxay caymiska aad aqbasho?

We accept most major insurance plans including Takaful, Amal, and various international providers. Please contact our billing department for specific coverage information.Waxaan aqbalnaa qorshayaasha caymiska ee ugu waaweyn oo ay ku jiraan Takaful, Amal, iyo kuwa caalamiga ah. Fadlan la xiriir qaybta lacag-bixinta.

How can I get my medical records?Sideen ku heli karaa diiwaanadayda?

You can request your medical records by visiting our medical records department with a valid ID. Processing takes 2-3 business days. You can also request records online.Waxaad dalban kartaa diiwaanada adiga oo booqanaya qaybta diiwaanada oo leh aqoonsi. Dhamaystirku waa 2-3 maalmood shaqo.

Is there a pharmacy in the hospital?Ma farmasi baa ku jirta isbitaalka?

Yes, we have a fully stocked pharmacy on the ground floor that operates 24/7. Our pharmacists can help you understand your medications.Haa, waxaan leenahay farmasi buuxa oo ku taal dhulka hoose oo shaqeysa 24/7.

What languages do your doctors speak?Luqado sideed buu ku hadlaa dhakhtarku?

Our doctors speak Somali, Arabic, English, and some speak Italian and Turkish. Interpreter services are also available upon request.Dhakhaatiiradayadu waxay ku hadlaan Soomaali, Carabi, Ingiriisi, iyo qaar baa ku hadla Talyaani iyo Turkiga.

Do you offer home visit services?Ma bixisaan adeegyo booqasho guriga?

Yes, we offer home visit services for patients who are unable to travel to the hospital. Please call our reception to schedule a home visit.Haa, waxaan bixinaa adeegyo booqasho guriga bukaanada aan awoodin inay yimaadaan isbitaalka. Fadlan soo wac reception-ka.

Still Have Questions?Weli Su'aalaa Leedahay?

Contact us directly and we'll be happy to help.Nala soo xiriir waxaanan ku caawin doonaa farxad.
